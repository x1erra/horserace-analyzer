Fair Grounds - New Orleans, LA  |  fairgroundsracecourse.com

Horses have been running at the Fair Grounds Race Course location since as early as 1838. The track features a one mile dirt track and a 7/8 mile turf course.
Fair Grounds' biggest stakes: Louisiana Derby, New Orleans Handicap, and Risen Star.
